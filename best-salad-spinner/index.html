A salad spinner is one of those kitchen tools that quietly transforms your meal prep. Instead of waiting for wet lettuce to drip-dry or patting each leaf with paper towels, a good spinner gets greens crisp and ready in seconds, and it’s just as handy for washing berries, herbs, or even freshly made pasta. When shopping, focus on three things: the pump or pull-cord mechanism (one-handed pumps are more convenient), capacity (how much salad you typically make at once), and stability (a non-slip base and sturdy lid matter). The best models balance ease of use, drying power, and counter-space footprint without breaking the bank.
